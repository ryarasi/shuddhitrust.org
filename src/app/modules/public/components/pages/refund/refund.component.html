Refund policy


This Refund Policy governs how Shuddhi Trust processes any potential request for refunds of donations.

  1. Our standard policy is to not offer refunds of the donations we receive.
  2. In case you wish to receive a refund because it was made in error or if you wish to change the amount or if you wish to receive a refund due to any emergency, please contact us within 72 hrs of the transaction. We may not be able to guarantee a favourable decision, but we will sincerely consider your situation and arrive at a decision.
  3. All decisions regarding your request for refund will be unilaterally settled by us and at our discretion.

Changes to this refund policy

Shuddhi Trust has the discretion to update this refund policy at any time without notice. We encourage Users to frequently check this page for any changes to stay informed about our policy regarding refunds. You acknowledge and agree that it is your responsibility to review these refund policy periodically and become aware of modifications.

Your acceptance of these terms

By donating to us, you signify your acceptance of this refund policy.

Contacting us

If you have any questions about this refund policy, the practices of this site, or your dealings with this site, please  contact us.
